# The Repair Deck: A Major Arcana for Mending

*Status: Grammar imported, 6/22 chapters in grammar format. Not yet drafted as prose.*

**Working thesis:** 69% of conflicts never resolve (Gottman). The question becomes repair capacity, not problem-solving. This book maps 22 archetypal repair patterns across intimate, communal, and civilizational scales.

**Structure:** 22 chapters (cards), each examined through 10 lenses (Research, Clinical, Spiritual, Practices, Rituals, Songs, Kids Version, Sci-Fi, Open Source, Cracks).

**First 6 cards drafted:**
1. The 69% That Never Gets Solved (Gottman, perpetual problems)
2. The Cry Beneath the Fight (Bowlby, attachment theory)
3. Hold Me Tight (Sue Johnson, EFT)
4. Making Amends (AA's 12-step model)
5. Staying Alive in the Fire (Linehan, DBT)
6. What Survives the Turning (ecological sustainability)

**Connection:** Extends Working Architecture (practical containers → repair when containers break). The repair grammar is what happens when the responsibility structures meet actual human mess.
