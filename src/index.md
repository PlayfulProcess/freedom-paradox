## How They Connect

**The Freedom Paradox** is the diagnosis (power without responsibility). **Grammars of the Living World** is the framework (what responsibility structures are). **The Species That Tells Stories** is the narrative heart (how stories carry adaptive wisdom). **Working Architecture** is the practical manual (how to build containers Monday morning). **The Campfire Stories** is the proof of concept (stories built using the three-filter test, for actual children, in actual bedtimes). **The Research Compendium** is the foundation they all stand on.

Each works alone. Together they form the full architecture.
