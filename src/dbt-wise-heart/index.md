# The Wise Heart: DBT Skills Through World Myths

*Status: Grammar imported, framework complete. Not yet drafted as prose.*

**Working thesis:** Every child needs emotional skills, and the world's oldest stories teach them better than lectures. This book teaches all 29 core DBT skills through cross-cultural myths and folktales for ages 4-8.

**Structure:** 4 modules, 29 story-skill pairs:
- **Module 1: The Quiet Eye** (Mindfulness) — 7 skills
- **Module 2: The Dark Wood** (Distress Tolerance) — 10 skills
- **Module 3: The Feeling Compass** (Emotion Regulation) — 8 skills
- **Module 4: The Talking Circle** (Interpersonal Effectiveness) — 5 skills

**Design principles:** No shame. The myth does the teaching. Mantras must be singable, repeatable, retrievable under stress. Every tradition is honored — no tradition is decoration.

**Connection:** Proof of concept #2 after Campfire Stories. If Campfire Stories proved stories carry adaptive wisdom, Wise Heart proves they can teach specific clinical skills.
