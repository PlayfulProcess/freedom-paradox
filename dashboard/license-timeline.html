The Pattern (Ch. 6)

Every license change followed the same arc: company changes license to block cloud providers from offering the software as a service. Community forks within weeks. Fork achieves escape velocity. Company either gets acquired or reverses course. But the fork continues regardless.

You can change your license back, but you cannot unfork a fork. The community that left has built its own home, and it has no reason to return.
