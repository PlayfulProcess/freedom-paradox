## TOP 10 INTEGRATIONS (by priority)

### 1. Khan Academy 3-4x acceleration (No Priors)
**Maps to:** Ch38 (What AI Actually Is) or Ch13 (The Freedom Paradox)
**Why HIGH:** Single strongest positive case for AI. Verified by Levitt. Book must engage honestly — acknowledge the genuine good while maintaining that the container matters (12-year companion replacing human relationship is different from tutoring tool).

### 2. Cotra's crunch-time argument (80K Hours)
**Maps to:** Ch38 (What AI Actually Is) + Ch39 (Grammar We Need)
**Why HIGH:** Strongest counter to intelligence-wisdom gap. "Every AI company's safety plan is: use AI to solve AI safety." Circular dependency the book should name.

### 3. Leike superalignment collapse (80K Hours)
**Maps to:** Ch09 (Safety Argument) + Ch39 (Grammar We Need)
**Why HIGH:** Real-time evidence that safety erodes under commercial pressure. Strengthens the helper's innocence argument already in Ch39.

### 4. Gopnik's gardener/carpenter (On Being)
**Maps to:** Ch16 (Oldest Technology) or Ch35 (Family as Grammar)
**Why HIGH:** Children as R&D division of humanity. Gardener model = provide conditions for unpredictable growth. Grandmother hypothesis. Strongest scientific evidence for the bedtime story thesis.

### 5. Linehan filter on indigenous ontologies (Emerald stress tests)
**Maps to:** Ch24 (Thesis) + methodology throughout
**Why HIGH:** Deepest challenge to the book's own epistemology. Filter positions Western evaluative framework as arbiter of non-Western validity. Response: filter governs author's claims, not indigenous systems themselves.

### 6. Menakem on racialized trauma as somatic (On Being)
**Maps to:** Ch14 (Nervous System) + Ch24 (Thesis) + [REPAIR DECK]
**Why HIGH:** White body supremacy rooted in 1000 years of European brutalization, not ideology. Vagus nerve as site where racialization lives. Challenge to Linehan filter: "compassionate does not mean making comfort the goal."

### 7. Andreotti "AI is Earth speaking back through silicon" (For the Wild)
**Maps to:** Ch38 (What AI Actually Is)
**Why HIGH:** Alternative hypothesis the book should hold. If training paradigm determines output, AI could amplify care. Both this and the structural critique may be simultaneously true.

### 8. SB 1047 veto + OpenAI erotica timing (Hard Fork)
**Maps to:** Ch09 (Safety Argument) + Ch13 (Freedom Paradox)
**Why HIGH:** Regulatory capture in real time. Governor vetoed safety bill under corporate lobby pressure. OpenAI released erotica features immediately after companion regulation passed. Power without responsibility dramatized.

### 9. Kimmerer on animate grammar (On Being)
**Maps to:** Ch24 (Thesis) + Ch26 (Axiom)
**Why HIGH:** English "it" applied to living beings encodes objectification. Potawatomi animate grammar extends to all living beings. Language-as-grammar that structures relationship to the living world.

### 10. Emerald stress tests (5 challenges)
**Maps to:** Preface or methodology note
**Why HIGH:** The book's own framework honestly tested. All five survivable with clarifications:
- Polyvagal as correlate, not foundation
- Adaptive frame needs unit-of-analysis specification
- Naming the trap of solving is itself solving (acknowledge recursion)
- Linehan filter governs author's claims, not indigenous systems
- Frameworks that survive contain their own disruption mechanisms
