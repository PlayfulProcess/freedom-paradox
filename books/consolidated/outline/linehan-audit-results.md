## CRITICAL FINDINGS (Must Address)

### 1. Adaptive frame masks moral claims (Ch24)
"Good and bad are moral categories... This book does not use them" — but the book absolutely makes moral arguments. Calling slavery "non-adaptive" rather than "evil" is technically accurate but ethically evacuated.
**Fix needed:** Acknowledge limits of the adaptive frame for irreversible harm to the powerless.

### 2. Extraction critique contradicts digital practice (Ch15 vs Ch21)
Ch15 critiques extracting contemplative practices from their relational context. Ch21 then instructs: draw a tarot card on your phone. The chapter acknowledges the gap but reframes it as acceptable.
**Fix needed:** Name the compromise honestly — "Living with the Gap" framing.

### 3. CPB dissolution stated as fact 4 times
Chs 33, 37, 39, 41 reference "CPB dissolved in 2026" as established. Needs verification or narrative framing.
**Fix needed:** Verify or clarify temporal stance.

### 4. Unverified financial claims in Ch37
Sesame $469M, Totoro $1.46B, Ghost $9M — all presented without sources in a chapter about governance accountability.
**Fix needed:** Add year and source for all financial figures, or note as approximate.

### 5. Citizens' assemblies causal overreach (Ch36)
"Better outcomes than elected officials" conflates recommendation quality with governance outcomes.
**Fix needed:** Distinguish deliberation quality from implementation outcomes.

## HIGH FINDINGS (Should Address)

### 6. Corporate actors treated with contempt (Ch4, Ch7, Ch8)
"Entirely by strategy" dismisses mixed motives. "Confession" language treats Zuckerberg as caught. Safety "theater" presented as certainty.
**Fix needed:** Acknowledge mixed motives; hedge theater interpretation; use "explanation" not "confession."

### 7. Memoir mixed with evidence (Ch35)
Personal anecdote ("are you upset with me?") functions as evidence rather than illustration.
**Fix needed:** Distinguish "what I learned" from "what the research shows."

### 8. Single-tradition recommendation may be inaccessible (Ch40)
Deep practice requires access to living communities. Not all readers have this.
**Fix needed:** Acknowledge access barriers.

### 9. recursive.eco presented as existing vs aspirational (Ch37)
Features described in present tense create confusion about what's built vs proposed.
**Fix needed:** Clarify what's live vs what's design intention.
