# The Quiet Eye: Mindfulness Skills

*Module*

## The Song

Quiet eye, open heart —
Seeing clearly is the start.

## For Parents

This module covers DBT Core Mindfulness — the foundation that all other skills build on. Linehan drew these skills from Zen Buddhism and contemplative traditions. The three What Skills (Observe, Describe, Participate) answer 'what do I do to be mindful?' The three How Skills (Non-Judgmentally, One-Mindfully, Effectively) answer 'how do I do it?' Wise Mind integrates everything. If you only work on one module, work on this one.

## About

Before you can handle big feelings, you need to SEE them. Mindfulness is the foundation — learning to pay attention to what's actually happening, inside and outside, without rushing to judge or fix. These seven stories teach the What Skills (Observe, Describe, Participate) and the How Skills (Non-Judgmentally, One-Mindfully, Effectively), all rooted in Wise Mind — the place where thinking and feeling meet.

## How to Use

Read one story per week. After each story, practice the 'Try This' for several days before moving to the next. The mantras ('The Song') are designed to be singable and retrievable — practice them until your child can recall them spontaneously.
