# The Feeling Compass: Emotion Regulation Skills

*Module*

## The Song

Feelings come like weather too —
I can feel AND choose what to do.

## For Parents

This module covers DBT Emotion Regulation — skills for understanding and changing unwanted emotions. Unlike Distress Tolerance (survive the crisis), these skills work on the longer arc: reducing vulnerability, checking interpretations, and acting opposite to unhelpful emotion urges. The ABC PLEASE skills (Build Mastery, Cope Ahead, PLEASE) are preventive. Check the Facts and Opposite Action are in-the-moment skills.

## About

Emotions are like weather — they come and go, and they're always telling you something. But sometimes the weather report is WRONG. Chicken Little thought the sky was falling. The Wind thought blowing harder would work. These eight stories teach how to understand your emotions, check whether they fit the facts, change them when they don't, accumulate good experiences, witness your feelings without drowning, solve problems creatively, and build a body and life that keeps you less vulnerable to emotional storms.

## How to Use

Start with Check the Facts (Chicken Little) — it's the most immediately practical. Build Mastery and PLEASE are daily practices. Opposite Action is powerful but requires the child to already have some mindfulness skill (they need to notice the urge before they can reverse it).
