# The Research Compendium

**The primary research behind the multi-book project by PlayfulProcess.**

This compendium organizes 48+ deep research files into thematic sections. Each file was generated through Claude-assisted deep research sessions as part of the [book-repo](https://github.com/PlayfulProcess/freedom-paradox) project, which includes:

- **The Freedom Paradox** — Open source confronts AI (16 chapters, ~85K words)
- **Grammars of the Living World** — What responsibility structures are (10 chapters, ~21K words)
- **The Species That Tells Stories** — How stories carry adaptive wisdom (7 chapters, in progress)
- **Working Architecture** — Building containers Monday morning (12 chapters, ~20K words)
- **The Campfire Stories** — Bedtime stories from cross-cultural traditions (5 books × 2 stories)

The research spans developmental psychology, invasion ecology, game theory, contemplative traditions, platform design, colonial studies, co-regulation science, and more.
