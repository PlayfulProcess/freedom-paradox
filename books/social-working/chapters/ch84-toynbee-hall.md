# Toynbee Hall

*Places_Of_Care*

## Reflection

What was generous in this model? What was condescending?

## Source

Samuel Barnett: 'The poor need not money but friendship.' Named after Arnold Toynbee, who died at 30 after working himself to exhaustion in service to the poor.

## Context

London, 1884. The model for all settlement houses. Jane Addams visited here before founding Hull House. Where university students lived alongside the poor to 'learn from' them—though the paternalism was embedded from the start.

## Archetypal Resonances

The Hierophant, The World, Temperance
