# Zumbi dos Palmares

## Reflection

What freedom was built 200 years before Hull House that history erased?

## Source

Palmares at its height had 20,000+ residents across multiple villages. Zumbi led guerrilla resistance for 15 years after the main quilombo fell. Captured and beheaded; his head displayed in public to 'prove he was not immortal.'

## Context

Last leader of Quilombo dos Palmares (1655-1695), the largest maroon settlement in the Americas. For nearly a century, Palmares resisted Portuguese colonizers—a self-organized community of escaped enslaved Africans and Indigenous peoples practicing mutual aid, collective governance, and armed resistance.
