# Maroon Communities

*Places_Of_Care*

## Reflection

What freedom can only be built by running away?

## Source

The word 'maroon' comes from Spanish 'cimarrón'—wild, untamed. Freedom required wildness.

## Context

Throughout the Americas, 1500s-present. Jamaica (Accompong), Suriname (Saramaka, Ndyuka), Florida (Black Seminoles), Brazil (quilombos). Self-liberated people building freedom in hostile territory.

## Archetypal Resonances

The Chariot, The Tower, The Star
