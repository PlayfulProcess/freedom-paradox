# Josephine Shaw Lowell

*Standard_Account*

## Reflection

How do we reckon with those who did both harm and good?

## Source

'A social Darwinist organizer of the COS movement, Lowell believed that poverty had its roots in the character of the poor. Pauperism would be eliminated by educating and rehabilitating the poor' rather than through relief (52).

## Context

'Founder of the New York City Charity Organization Society' (52) and close Addams ally through the Anti-Imperialist League and National Consumers League. Rafter documents her 'central role in advancing eugenic criminology policies' that have been 'downplayed or erased in accounts of her life' (50).

## Archetypal Resonances

Judgement, The High Priestess, Temperance
