# Sir Francis Galton

*Standard_Account*

## Reflection

What words do we coin that will be condemned?

## Source

'Eugenic framings of 'fitness' were those characteristics already long exalted within moral economies of human value; those considered unfit were diverse members of oppressed groups' (35).

## Context

'First articulated eugenic doctrine in 1865 and later coined the actual word in 1883' (34). 'Eugenics became an incredibly popular ideology in which those considered 'fit' were encouraged to reproduce while those considered 'unfit' were discouraged from reproducing' (34).
