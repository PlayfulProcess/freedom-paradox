# Janie Porter Barrett

*Erased_Voices*

## Reflection

What grows from care that refuses institutional recognition?

## Source

Chapman & Withers (Chapter 2): Barrett's settlement is routinely left out of social work history. Chapter 2 documents how 'white social work history' erases Black settlement houses that were doing the same work—or earlier.

## Context

Founded Locust Street Settlement in Hampton, Virginia (1890)—possibly predating Hull House's breadth of services. Started in her own home, grew to community center serving Black families.

## Key Insight

Locust Street Settlement may have predated some of Addams's services, yet Barrett is rarely credited in 'standard account' textbooks.
