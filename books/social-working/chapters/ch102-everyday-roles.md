# Everyday Roles

## Reflection

What role are you playing that no institution recognizes?

## Source

Eisler's insight: in partnership societies, individual names didn't survive—only ROLES. The Chalice and the Blade.

## Description

The roles that do social working without credentials: Grandmother, Mother, Friend, Doula, Neighbor, Child. As Bayo Akomolafe says: 'Decolonization might be more about grandmothers than about systems.'
