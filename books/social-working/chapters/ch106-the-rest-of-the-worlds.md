# The Rest of the Worlds

## Reflection

What would it mean to learn social working from fungi?

## Description

More-than-human and beyond-profession social working. The Kingdoms (Animal, Plant, Mineral, Fungi), The Elements (Water, Fire, Air, Earth), Non-Duality (Spirit/Matter), Epistemologies (Science/Religion), and Contemporary Witnesses who point beyond the colonial frame. Social working was happening for billions of years before humans named it.
