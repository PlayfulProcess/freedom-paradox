# Freedom Schools

*Places_Of_Care*

## Reflection

What education is worth dying for?

## Source

Curriculum asked: 'What do we mean by freedom?' Three civil rights workers—Chaney, Goodman, Schwerner—murdered that summer.

## Context

Mississippi, 1964. Freedom Summer. 41 schools, 2,000+ students. Black history, constitutional rights, leadership. Education as liberation while registering voters.
