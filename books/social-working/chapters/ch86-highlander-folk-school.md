# Highlander Folk School

*Places_Of_Care*

## Reflection

What happens when a place teaches what the state forbids?

## Source

Myles Horton: 'We don't teach people. We help them learn.' The FBI tried to shut it down; the state seized the property in 1961.

## Context

Tennessee, 1932. Where Rosa Parks trained before Montgomery. Where MLK, John Lewis, and countless civil rights organizers learned. Desegregated decades before the law required it.
