# Huey Newton

*Erased_Voices*

## Reflection

What does it mean to feed people as an act of revolution?

## Source

The FBI called the Free Breakfast Program 'the greatest threat to internal security.' This was social work—meeting immediate needs—but framed as terrorism because it built Black community power.

## Context

Co-founded Black Panther Party. Created Free Breakfast for Children—the model for federal school breakfast programs. Also free health clinics, schools, pest control. 'Survival pending revolution.'

## Key Insight

The Panthers did what settlement houses claimed to do—feed children, provide healthcare, build community—but were criminalized for it. Same work, different bodies, different response.
