# J.J. Kelso

*Standard_Account*

## Reflection

Whose children are 'saved'? From what? For whom?

## Source

Part of the broader COS campaign: McCulloch's answer to 'What can we do?' included 'Third, we must get hold of the children' (35). Child apprehension emerged alongside eugenic reasoning about preventing 'unfit' reproduction.

## Context

'An architect of Canada's Children's Aid Society system' (35). Nancy Christie documents how 'Kelso aimed to convert as many Catholic children to Protestantism as possible. This practice was articulated as motivated by concern for the child' (35).

## Archetypal Resonances

The Chariot, The Fool, The Emperor
