# Elizabeth Williams Shadd Shreve

*Erased_Voices*

## Reflection

Whose knowledge sustains communities while remaining unwritten?

## Source

Chapman & Withers (Chapter 2): Shreve represents the most invisible layer—not only erased as a Black woman doing community care, but invisible even within the recovery of erased voices.

## Context

Circuit-riding missionary serving the Buxton fugitive settlement in Canada. Sister of Mary Ann Shadd Cary. Nearly invisible to history—even within the erasure of Black social workers.

## Key Insight

If Ida B. Wells is footnoted and Mary Ann Shadd Cary is nearly forgotten, Shreve is truly invisible. How many layers of erasure exist?
