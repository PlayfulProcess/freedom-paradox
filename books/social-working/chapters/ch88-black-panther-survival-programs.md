# Black Panther Survival Programs

*Places_Of_Care*

## Reflection

What if feeding children is more revolutionary than rhetoric?

## Source

Huey Newton: 'Survival pending revolution.' The FBI called the Free Breakfast Program 'the greatest threat to internal security.'

## Context

Oakland and nationwide, 1966-1980s. Free breakfast for children. Free health clinics. Free ambulance services. Free shoes. Free pest control. Revolution through meeting immediate needs.
