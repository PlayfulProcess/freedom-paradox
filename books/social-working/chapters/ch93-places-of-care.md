# Places of Care

## Reflection

What places in your life have been sites of care? Which were destroyed?

## Description

Places where social working happened: Quilombo dos Palmares (1605), Toynbee Hall (1884), Hull House (1889), Highlander Folk School (1932), Freedom Schools (1964), Black Panther programs (1966), Terreiros (ongoing), Catholic Worker houses (1933), Maroon communities (throughout), and Africville (destroyed 1970). Some were built by professionals; most by those escaping oppression.
