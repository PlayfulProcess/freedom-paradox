# Viola Desmond

*Erased_Voices*

## Reflection

What joy is an act of resistance?

## Source

Now on the Canadian $10 bill—but Chapman & Withers use her story to show how Canadian 'national mythology' claims Canada is 'not a racist country, or at least less so than our southern neighbour,' which 'is untrue' (67).

## Context

'Often referred to as Canada's Rosa Parks, was jailed and eventually fined after sitting in a section reserved for whites in the Roseland,' a movie theatre in New Glasgow, Nova Scotia (74). 'A decade before Colvin and Parks wouldn't move from their seats in Alabama' (74).
