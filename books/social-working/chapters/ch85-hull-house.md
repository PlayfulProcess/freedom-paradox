# Hull House

*Places_Of_Care*

## Reflection

Can a place do both harm and good? What does that mean?

## Source

Chapman & Withers: 'We should neither demonize nor romanticize Addams' (46). Hull House did real good AND participated in interlocking systems of oppression.

## Context

Chicago, 1889. Jane Addams and Ellen Gates Starr. The most famous American settlement. Kindergarten, English classes, arts programs. Also: eugenic health screenings, marriage certificate requirements, complicity.

## Archetypal Resonances

The Empress, The Hierophant, The Tower
