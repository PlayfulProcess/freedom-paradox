# Terreiros

*Places_Of_Care*

## Reflection

What care continues despite centuries of criminalization?

## Source

During COVID, terreiros fed communities when government failed. Centuries of criminalization couldn't stop them.

## Context

Brazil, ongoing. Candomblé and Umbanda temples. Community centers where Axé flows. Food, healing, belonging, justice. Often in the poorest neighborhoods, doing what the state refuses.

## Archetypal Resonances

The High Priestess, The Empress, The World
