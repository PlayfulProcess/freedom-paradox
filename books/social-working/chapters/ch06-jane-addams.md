# Jane Addams

*Standard_Account*

## Reflection

What violences hide behind moral authority?

## Source

Chapman & Withers argue we should neither 'demonize nor romanticize' Addams—she was both radical AND complicit. 'Our point isn't that we should find another flawless historical figure to emulate' (46).

## Context

Hull-House founder, Nobel laureate, early NAACP member who spoke against lynching. Yet also 'called on governments to insist upon health certificates from the applicant for a marriage license' and was 'involved in plans to institutionalize women who had been labelled as feebleminded' (Chapman & Withers, 51). Seconded the 1912 presidential nomination of eugenicist Theodore Roosevelt.
