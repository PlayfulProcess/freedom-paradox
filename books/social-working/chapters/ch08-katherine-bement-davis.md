# Katherine Bement Davis

*Standard_Account*

## Reflection

What fears drive policies that claim to be rational?

## Source

Davis 'may well have tended towards the culturally determinist end of eugenic doctrine, but that doesn't place her outside of eugenics' (50). Worked with Addams in 'various social workings, including radical political struggles' (51).

## Context

Bedford Hills Reformatory superintendent. Chapman & Withers cite Nicole Rafter's work documenting Davis's 'central role in incarcerating women on eugenic grounds' (50). Close ally of Addams—'If Jane Addams were President, Katharine Davis would be her Vice President' (51).

The Moon, The Tower, Judgement
