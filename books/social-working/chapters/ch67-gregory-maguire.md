# Gregory Maguire

*Wicked_History*

## Reflection

Whose perspective makes the villain?

## Source

'People are so rarely what they seem.' The moral: wickedness is constructed by those in power.

## Context

Wicked: The Life and Times of the Wicked Witch of the West (1995). Told the story from the 'villain's' perspective. Elphaba wasn't wicked—she was green, different, fighting oppression.

## Archetypal Resonances

The Hermit, The High Priestess, Justice
