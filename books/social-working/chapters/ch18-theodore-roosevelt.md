# Theodore Roosevelt

*Standard_Account*

## Reflection

What does 'progress' require? Whose bodies?

## Source

Chapman & Withers (47): Addams seconded his nomination despite his eugenics. The 'Progressive' label hid the eugenic core—progress for whom?

## Context

Eugenicist president whose 1912 Progressive Party nomination Jane Addams seconded. 'Vice President Roosevelt became president in 1901 after McKinley was assassinated... made an unsuccessful run for the presidency in 1912, representing the Progressive Party, which he'd founded' (Chapman & Withers, 47).

## Key Insight

Addams—celebrated progressive, early NAACP member—seconded a eugenicist's nomination. This is the 'neither demonize nor romanticize' point made flesh.
