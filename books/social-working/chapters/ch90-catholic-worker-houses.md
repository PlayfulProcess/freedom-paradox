# Catholic Worker Houses

*Places_Of_Care*

## Reflection

What if you gave up the right to judge who deserves help?

## Source

Dorothy Day: 'The Gospel takes away our right to discriminate between the deserving and the undeserving poor.'

## Context

New York and worldwide, 1933-present. Dorothy Day and Peter Maurin. Hospitality houses, soup kitchens, farms. Voluntary poverty. Anarchism meets Catholicism.

## Archetypal Resonances

The Hermit, The Empress, The Fool
