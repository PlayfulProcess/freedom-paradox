# Quilombo dos Palmares

*Places_Of_Care*

## Reflection

What if the first 'settlement house' was built by those escaping enslavement?

## Source

Population estimates range from 10,000 to 30,000 across multiple villages. Practiced African-derived agriculture, governance, and spirituality. Resisted Portuguese military expeditions for nine decades.

## Context

The first large-scale settlement of care in the Americas (1605-1694). Self-organized community of escaped enslaved Africans, Indigenous peoples, and others fleeing colonial violence. Collective farming, shared governance, spiritual practices. Nearly 200 years before Hull House.
