# Charlie Smalls

*Wicked_History*

## Reflection

What Black genius is celebrated then forgotten?

## Source

The Wiz reimagined Kansas as Harlem, the Yellow Brick Road as Black cultural expression. 7 Tony Awards. Then erasure.

## Context

Composer/lyricist of The Wiz (1975). Won the Tony for Best Original Score. 'Ease on Down the Road.' Died in 1987, largely forgotten.

## Archetypal Resonances

The Magician, The Wheel of Fortune, The Hermit
