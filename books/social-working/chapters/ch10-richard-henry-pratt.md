# Richard Henry Pratt

*Standard_Account*

## Reflection

What bondage presents itself as education?

## Source

'A great general has said that the only good Indian is a dead one… In a sense, I agree with the sentiment, but only in this: that all the Indian there is in the race should be dead. Kill the Indian in him, and save the man' (60).

## Context

Founded Carlisle Indian Industrial School, 'the first of its kind' (60). Spoke at the 1892 National Conference on Charities and Corrections—the organization that 'directly became legitimized professional social work' (60).

## Archetypal Resonances

The Devil, The Tower, Death
