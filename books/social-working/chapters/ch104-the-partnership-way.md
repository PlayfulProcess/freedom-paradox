# The Partnership Way

## Reflection

What would social working look like without named heroes—but with remembered places?

## Source

Riane Eisler (1987): dominator vs. partnership models. The chalice (partnership) vs. the blade (domination). Places where partnership was practiced.

## Description

Eisler's insight from The Chalice and the Blade: the opposite of patriarchy isn't matriarchy—it's partnership (gylany). In partnership societies, ROLES survived, not names. The Grandmother, not Jane Addams. And PLACES of care—Quilombo dos Palmares (1605) predates Hull House by nearly 200 years.
