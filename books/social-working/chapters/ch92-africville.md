# Africville

*Places_Of_Care*

## Reflection

What communities have been destroyed in the name of improvement?

## Source

Chapman & Withers: Albert Rose visited for 'two hours' and wrote the report justifying destruction (69). Pa Carvery held out until 1970.

## Context

Halifax, Nova Scotia, 1848-1970. Black community destroyed by 'urban renewal.' Denied city services for decades, then demolished for being a 'slum.' Residents relocated in garbage trucks.
