# James Baldwin

*Erased_Voices*

## Reflection

What are you refusing to face?

## Quote

Not everything that is faced can be changed, but nothing can be changed until it is faced.

## Source

Chapman & Withers' framework: Baldwin represents those who did 'social working' through testimony and truth-telling. His witness—naming what America refused to face—was a form of care.

## Context

Writer, witness, exile. Left America to survive. Returned to testify. Gay, Black, unflinching—and therefore erased from mainstream civil rights narratives.

## Key Insight

Witness
