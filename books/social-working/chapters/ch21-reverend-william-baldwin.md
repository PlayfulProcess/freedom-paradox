# Reverend William Baldwin

*Standard_Account*

## Reflection

What would it mean to 'get rid of' poverty without getting rid of the poor?

## Source

'To keep them in comfortable quarters and allow them to live in idleness is not the way to get rid of them. Hard work and strict discipline… [are] the best means of getting rid of the professional vagrant' (34). A report summarizing his statement called poor people an 'army of tramps that infest this Province' (34).

## Context

Representing Toronto's Associated Charities (COS), Baldwin 'clearly articulated the movement's drive to eradicate poor people' (34).
