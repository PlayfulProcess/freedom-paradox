# Reverdy Cassius Ransom

*Erased_Voices*

## Reflection

What wisdom comes from standing apart from mainstream success?

## Source

Chapman & Withers (Chapter 2): Ransom's Institutional Church was doing settlement work contemporaneous with Hull House—but from a Black church tradition. The 'standard account' credits Addams while erasing Ransom.

## Context

AME Bishop who founded Institutional Church in Chicago (1900)—combining worship with social services, employment bureau, kindergarten. Christian socialist who brought social gospel to Black churches.

## Key Insight

The Black church as social work institution predates secular white settlement houses—but this history is invisible in textbooks.
