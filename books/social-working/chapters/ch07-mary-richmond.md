# Mary Richmond

*Standard_Account*

## Reflection

When does structure become a cage?

## Source

Of poor fathers she called 'married vagabonds': 'To treat the married vagabond as not responsible, is only to increase his irresponsibility' (32). The book argues she was liberal, not conservative—like 'most social work done today' (32).

## Context

Created 'scientific casework' to differentiate professional practice from volunteer work. Believed 'social conditions could be wonderfully improved if, to every family in distress, could be sent a volunteer visitor' (Chapman & Withers, 31). Though she grew up a poor orphan, 'much of Richmond's work denigrates poor people as morally reprehensible' (31).
