# Sanitized Icons

## Reflection

What parts of your heroes have been sanitized?

## Description

Highly visible figures whose complexity is hidden. MLK's radical economic critique is sanitized into 'I Have a Dream.' Gandhi's caste contradictions disappear. Malcolm X becomes a t-shirt. Chapman & Withers (28-29): 'We want Gandhi, Goldman, King, and Malcolm X to be celebrated for their important work... But celebrating their work isn't best facilitated by positioning them as always and only on the side of justice.'
