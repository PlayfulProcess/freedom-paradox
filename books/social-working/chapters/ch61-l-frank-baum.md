# L. Frank Baum

*Wicked_History*

## Reflection

What genocides hide behind our beloved stories?

## Source

Two Aberdeen Saturday Pioneer editorials (1890) advocated 'the total annihilation of the few remaining Indians' after Wounded Knee.

## Context

The Wonderful Wizard of Oz (1900). Created the canonical American fairy tale. Also wrote editorials calling for the extermination of Native Americans.

## Archetypal Resonances

The Magician, The Fool, The World
