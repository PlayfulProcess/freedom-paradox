# Aaron 'Pa' Carvery

*Erased_Voices*

## Reflection

What does it mean to hold ground when the world insists you move?

## Source

Chapman & Withers describe how 'many people in Africville were against relocation and the destruction of their community,' contrary to Rose's claims that residents were 'ready and to some extent eager' (70).

## Context

'The relocations took place from 1964 to 1967, with the final holdout, 'Pa' Carvery, vacating his home in 1970' (70). While Albert Rose received medals, Carvery held his ground.
