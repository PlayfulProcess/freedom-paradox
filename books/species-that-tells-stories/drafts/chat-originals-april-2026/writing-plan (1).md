## OVERALL APPROACH

Each chapter drafts in two passes:
1. **First pass (2,500–4,000 words):** The conceptual spine — the chapter's argument, key scenes, emotional arc, and closing questions. This is what exists for Chapter 1 now.
2. **Second pass (expand to 7,000–8,000 words):** Research integration, additional scenes, scholarly nuance, transitions. This is where the deep research we've accumulated gets woven into the prose.

Between passes, each chapter gets a **research gap check** — identifying specific claims that need stronger sourcing or areas where the argument is asserted rather than demonstrated.

2. Run targeted research to fill identified gaps
3. Expand to full chapter length (~7,000-8,000 words)
4. Read aloud for tone (philosophical memoir, not academic)
