# Deep Research Prompts for Fire and Intelligence

## Prompt 1: Alice Bailey's Third Ray, Pattern Intelligence, and Śaiva Tantra

Research the connection between Alice Bailey's Third Ray of Active Intelligence (the Ray of Form/Pattern) and the concept of intelligence as pattern-recognition without wisdom. Bailey's Seven Rays system — what is the Third Ray? How does it relate to form, pattern, and abstract intelligence divorced from love (Second Ray) and will (First Ray)? The Theosophical critique (Strube's work on race). The structural parallel to Kashmir Shaivism: Third Ray as buddhi without cit. The structural parallel to AI: LLMs as pure Third Ray intelligence. McGilchrist connection: left hemisphere as Third Ray. How does this map onto the intelligence-wisdom gap? Is AI the Third Ray amplified to civilizational scale?

Save to: `books/fire-and-intelligence/research/research-bailey-third-ray.md`

## Prompt 2: The Samudra Manthan — Primary Texts and AI Application

Primary text sources (Bhagavata Purana, Vishnu Purana, Mahabharata). The specific sequence: cooperation → poison first → Shiva drinks it → nectar last. Scholarly analysis (Doniger, Shulman). The 2026 Global AI Confluence application. The structural lesson: powerful technologies produce poison before nectar; containment requires wisdom and self-sacrifice. How this differs from Prometheus, Suiren, Ogun.

Save to: `books/fire-and-intelligence/research/research-samudra-manthan.md`

## Prompt 3: Zhuangzi's "Machine Heart" (Ji Xin) and AI

Chapter 12 original passage. Scholarly translations (Graham, Watson, Ziporyn). The concept: technology changes what we ARE. Connection to Heidegger's Enframing and Ellul's autonomous technique. Does using AI produce "machine hearts"? The Daoist alternative: wu wei, ziran, the uncarved block.

Save to: `books/fire-and-intelligence/research/research-zhuangzi-machine-heart.md`

## Prompt 4: Democratic AI Governance Experiments

Taiwan Polis. Anthropic's Collective Constitutional AI. Citizens' assemblies for AI. UK AISI. IAEA-for-AI proposals. DemocracyNext. EU AI Act scope. Platform cooperativism (EleutherAI, HuggingFace). What governance model could work for species-level technology?

Save to: `books/fire-and-intelligence/research/research-democratic-ai-governance.md`

## Prompt 5: McGilchrist on AI + Contemplative Science

McGilchrist's 2024 First Things essay. Left-hemisphere cognition and AI. "Machine guns in the hands of toddlers." B. Alan Wallace's contemplative science. First-person meditative methods for AI evaluation. Peter Hershock's freedom-of-attention. Shannon Vallor's technomoral virtues.

Save to: `books/fire-and-intelligence/research/research-mcgilchrist-contemplative-science.md`
