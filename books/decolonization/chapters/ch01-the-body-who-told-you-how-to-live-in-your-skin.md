# The Body: Who Told You How to Live in Your Skin?

*Domains*

## About

Colonization begins in the body. It reshapes what you eat, how you heal, when you rest, how you move, what you consider beautiful, and how you are born and die. The colonial body is a body optimized for production — disciplined, standardized, measured against norms invented in European capitals and exported as universal truth. Decolonizing the body means noticing whose rhythms you move to, whose medicine you trust, whose beauty standards live in your mirror.

## The Invitation

Before you try to reclaim anything, notice what lives in your body that isn't yours. Not to purge it — you can't — but to know it's there. The body remembers what the mind forgets.

## The Colonial Logic

The body as machine. The body as labor unit. The body as something to be civilized, corrected, covered, controlled. Colonial medicine replaces grandmother knowledge with pharmaceutical patents. Colonial agriculture replaces milpa with monoculture. Colonial beauty replaces a thousand forms of beauty with one.
