## Editorial follow-ups (light to medium)

The content was copied verbatim. None of the five chapters reference each other
by v3 numbering inside the text (verified by reading the openings). Light
copyedits the author may want at her next pass:

1. **ch01b — Glasses and Grief**: the chapter currently introduces Gottman cold. In v5's position (after Cracks interlude), it might want a one-paragraph hand-off from the Cracks register into the Gottman register. Optional — the chapter stands alone.
2. **ch04b — Daughter's Itch**: positioned mid-retreat in v5's structure. The chapter opens *"It was Day One when she interrupted a friend"* — the Day One reference is to the retreat. Already fits the surrounding chapters (Threshold → Daughter's Itch → Śūnya works as a sequence).
3. **ch05a — Unowned Dimension**: needs a check that its theme doesn't repeat what Śūnya proper already covers — possible overlap on the void/no-self material. May need a quarter-page distillation if redundancy shows.
4. **ch09a — Morning She Could Not Rise**: paired naturally with Deha Returning. No cross-references to other v3 chapters in the body.
5. **ch12a — Practice She Did Not Enter**: positioned between Composting and Right Size. The refusal sequence makes the right-sizing earned rather than aspirational.
