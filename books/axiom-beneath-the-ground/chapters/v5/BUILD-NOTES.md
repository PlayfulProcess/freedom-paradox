## Themes newly introduced in v5 (beyond v4's reach)

These threads were requested by the author during the v5 build and are now load-bearing in Ch03 §VII and Ch10 §VII:

1. **Technology is accelerating time.** The engagement-internet compresses the interval between impulse and response. Compressed intervals produce overconceptual selves, disconnected from the relational field.
2. **Social media is a karma accelerator.** The platform does not generate the pattern; the platform plays the pattern back at the speed capital requires. The tradition's technical sense of *karma* (pattern self-reinforcing) describes what the engagement-internet has made operational at scale.
3. **The collective karma of overindividualistic consumerism** primed the nervous system for engagement-scrolling before the engagement-internet existed. The platforms amplified a signal the culture had been generating for centuries.
4. **East vs. West, not as two cultures arguing, but as incompatible theories of personhood.** Autonomous individual consumer vs. local contraction of a relational field. The filter applies: one theory has passed the long-term test, the other is failing it.
5. **The ghost-model as structural anti-form of the engagement-internet.** Federation over ActivityPub makes the platform a service rather than a gate; the writer owns the audience. The book now explicitly holds recursive.eco inside this frame.

These themes accumulate across Ch03 and Ch10 without being lectured at the reader. They arrive as the narrator's *recognitions*, not as arguments imposed on a scene.
