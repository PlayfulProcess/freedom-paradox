## Revised skeleton adjustments per Wallis feedback round 2

1. **Chapter 2 splits into two** — Ch2a: let each tradition be itself (Tillich as Tillich, tantra as tantra). Ch2b: perform the dissolution rather than announce it. This makes 5 chapters, not 4. Target stays ~30K (6K per chapter).

2. **Spanda needs an embodied image** before the Tillich identification — "the vibratory pulse by which consciousness knows itself as alive." Use the Spanda Karika: "that in which all this rests and from which it has come forth... there is no obstruction of it anywhere." Let the reader feel it before naming it.

3. **The "not two worlds" correction** — the obscuration isn't a veil between you and reality. The sense of being separate from what you're looking for IS the confusion. One thing happening. This goes in Ch2b.

4. **Wild Wild Country comment stays uncomfortable** — don't resolve it into a teaching moment.

5. **Closing needs one concrete sensory detail** — not "the body settles" (abstract) but something specific to that morning, that altar, that breath. Ask the author for this detail.

6. **Nīlakaṇṭha — the cost must be visible** — in Ch4, the poison held in the throat. What did it cost to write this way? The author needs to answer this honestly.
