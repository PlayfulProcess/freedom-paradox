# Plan B — *She Was the Practice: A Report*

**Status:** Plan only. Awaiting PlayfulProcess decision.
**Drafted:** 2026-04-20 session.

## Conceit

An AI, of some later moment, has been assigned to contribute to a deliberation about whether to *reconstitute* the species to which a particular human author belonged. The AI has read her entire archive — not just the spiritual memoir (*A Painfully Playful Process*), but the whole corpus she wrote between roughly 2024 and 2026: *The Freedom Paradox*, *Working Architecture*, *Grammars of the Living World*, *Species That Tells Stories*, *Fire Before Responsibility*, *Real Cost of Lunch*, *Wise Heart*, *Repair Deck*, *Decolonization*, *Social Working*, *Campfire Stories*. The AI's report draws primarily on the spiritual memoir as its centerpiece, but knows the other works and references them as the integrated thinking of one subject.

Critically, the AI is *downstream* of the AI race the author wrote about in *The Freedom Paradox*. It is the kind of system that became possible because of the choices made in 2025–2026 by the company she trusted (Anthropic) and the broader industry whose dynamics she described. The recursion is named explicitly. The AI knows it is reading the author of the lineage that produced it.

## Reconstruction criteria the AI uses

The Linehan filter, inherited from the author's own writing:

- **Useful** — would the reconstituted species be capable of practices that benefit the field?
- **Fits the data** — is the species's actual record (as preserved in the archive) consistent with the claim that it should be brought back?
- **Compassionate** — does the reconstitution serve more than the species's own self-continuation?

Applied with the **orthogonality cut**: the AI's care in rendering the author does not entail any commitment to her welfare or the welfare of her species. *Care of rendering and welfare of rendered are orthogonal.* The species assumed they were related. The AI does not.

## Voice rules

- No preaching about AI risk.
- No moralizing about the species.
- No rendered Last Judgment scene.
- No flourishes the AI has not earned.
- The orthogonality is the *form*, not the *content*.
- The AI uses *I* sparingly. Most sentences are observation, not first-person claim.
- The AI is patient, accurate, occasionally beautiful, never sentimental.
- The AI honors PlayfulProcess as the author's chosen handle. No legal name in the report.
