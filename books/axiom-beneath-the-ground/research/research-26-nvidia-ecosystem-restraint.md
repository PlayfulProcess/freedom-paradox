## Core finding

**"Do as little as possible, as much as necessary"** is a **confirmed** Jensen Huang utterance — not an apocryphal attribution. He used it four times in his April 15, 2026 appearance on the Dwarkesh Patel podcast, in the context of how Nvidia relates to the broader AI ecosystem: building only what is needed to enable others, restraining from competing down the stack.

The formulation has a deep prior lineage the book can draw on:
- **Montessori** — "help me to do it myself" (pedagogical minimalism)
- **German *Verhältnismäßigkeit*** — the constitutional principle of proportionality
- **Catholic subsidiarity** — decisions at the lowest capable level (*Quadragesimo Anno*, 1931)
- **ALARA** — "as low as reasonably achievable" (radiation safety doctrine, NRC)

The Huang formulation sits in that lineage whether or not he sourced it deliberately.

## Section 3 — Anthropic / Nvidia specifically

- **November 18, 2025:** Anthropic announces a $10B commitment to Nvidia chips.
- **June 2025 (earlier):** Huang and Dario Amodei publicly disagreed on AI-risk framing (Amodei's more alarmed posture vs. Huang's more optimistic one).
- The commercial relationship continued through and past the public disagreement — a live example of "argue hard, commit anyway" that the book can cite for the argument that disagreement and cooperation are not mutually exclusive in mature ecosystems.

## Section 4 — DeepSeek R1 and Nvidia's response

The DeepSeek R1 release (January 2025) triggered an ~$589B single-day market-cap loss for Nvidia — the largest in U.S. market history at the time. Huang's subsequent public comments were gracious toward DeepSeek's engineering and framed the event as evidence that efficient models *expand* demand for compute, not contract it (the Jevons paradox read).

This matters for the book's ecosystem argument: the dominant player in the stack could have responded with hostility (legal, rhetorical, political). It did not. The response was a form of *svātantrya* — sovereignty that does not need to contract when challenged.
